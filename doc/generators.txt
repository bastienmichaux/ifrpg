"use random generation to create
what an army of programmers/writers would do for your project"

world structure:
world > regions (wilderness)
region > locations (village,town,dungeon,lair,fortress,cave,etc)
location > places (street,city gate,place,park)
place > buildings
building > rooms

containers:
- content f (what the owner has, would store in it)
- lockpick/traps: f (how precious content is for the owner)
